RazorWear™ Privacy Policy

Developer: Jacob Brown
Publisher: TraceWear™

Last updated: May 31, 2026

No Data Collection

RazorWear™ does not collect personal data. It does not collect your name, email address, location, device ID, browsing history, files, or usage analytics.

No Internet Transmission

RazorWear™ does not send data to a server. It does not include ads, telemetry, analytics, or third-party trackers.

Local Cleanup Logs

RazorWear™ may create local text logs on your device so you can review cleanup activity. These logs stay on your computer and are not uploaded.

Cleanup Scope

RazorWear™ is designed to clean temporary files only. It does not intentionally delete personal files from Desktop, Documents, Downloads, Pictures, Music, Videos, or OneDrive.
