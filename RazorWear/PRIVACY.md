# Privacy Policy

RazorWear does not collect personal data.

## What RazorWear Does

- Scans local temporary folders on your Windows PC.
- Deletes old temporary files only when you choose clean mode.
- Saves a local text log in the `logs` folder.

## What RazorWear Does Not Do

- Does not create an account.
- Does not collect your name, email, files, browsing history, device ID, or location.
- Does not send data to a server.
- Does not run in the background.
- Does not install a tracker, updater, browser extension, or service.

## Local Logs

RazorWear logs stay on your own computer. They are only there so you can see what the cleanup did or skipped.
