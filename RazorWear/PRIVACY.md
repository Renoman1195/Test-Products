# Privacy Policy

RazorWear does not collect personal data.

## What RazorWear Does

- Scans local temporary folders on your Windows PC.
- Deletes old temporary files only when you choose clean mode.
- Saves a local text log under `%LOCALAPPDATA%\TraceWear\RazorWear\logs`.
- Checks Microsoft updates only when you choose an update-check action.

## What RazorWear Does Not Do

- Does not create an account.
- Does not collect your name, email, files, browsing history, device ID, or location.
- Does not send cleanup data, logs, or personal data to a server.
- Does not run in the background.
- Does not install a tracker, browser extension, background updater, or service.

## Local Logs

RazorWear logs stay on your own computer. They are only there so you can see what the cleanup did or skipped.

## Update Checks

RazorWear can open or check Microsoft Windows Update and Microsoft Store app updates when you choose those actions. These checks use Microsoft update tools and do not transmit RazorWear cleanup logs or personal files.
